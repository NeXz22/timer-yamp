Welcome to your new favourite time-tracking mob-programming tool: YAMP!

The goal of mob programming is to improve collaboration, knowledge sharing, and productivity within a team. It allows team members to learn from each other and share their expertise, and it allows the team to move quickly and make progress on tasks. It also helps to ensure that all team members are on the same page and working towards the same goals.

YAMP is designed to support the mob programming process, making it easy for teams to track the time, goals and participants of a mob-programming session. With an intuitive interface and no required login- or registration process, you can share your individual mob-programming-session with all your teammates.
